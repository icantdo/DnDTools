Lolth's Fang

Dagger (1d4)

The blade seeps poison and burns to hold, only Lolth's chosen Driders can wield it, otherwise you take 2d10 necrotic damage

  • Fueled by hatred: Add +2 to attacks hit and damage rolls done with this weapon
  • Goddess' Venom: the dagger deals an extra 5d6 damage, the damage type is poison, necrotic or radiant, you choose which type when attacking
  • Burning Venom: after each strike, the target must make a constitution against your spellsave DC, on a fail it is magically poisoned for 1 minute...
  • Drider's might: If the dagger is wielded by a drider in their true form, the dagger's damage characteristic is instead 2D8
