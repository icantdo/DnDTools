# 1. Combat & UI Management

This section is the showcase of the platform. When users open the app, they should see a smooth and polished combat management experience.

---

## UI Polish & Bloodied Mechanic

### What We Will Do:

We will modernize the Streamlit interface. The most logical approach is to add **three different themes**:

* **Tech / Cyberpunk theme** (for futuristic settings)
* **Old School theme** (classic tabletop vibe)
* **Simple theme** (clean and minimal)

We will visually highlight the **is_bloodied** state (when HP drops below half) using elements like:

* Red border around character card
* Blood drop icon
* Visual emphasis indicators

We will also add:

* Action counter
* Bonus Action counter
* Reaction counter

### Why We’re Doing It:

During combat, the DM (Dungeon Master) must be able to quickly read the screen and instantly see who still has what available.

---

### Tier Distribution

**Free:**

* Standard UI
* Basic HP and initiative tracking
* Standard counters

**Member:**

* Exclusive dark/light themes
* Visual dice animations
* Advanced combat filters

## Dynamic Combat Narrator (Combat Logger)

### What We Will Do:

We will log background damage and status changes during combat.
At the end of the fight, we’ll use AI to generate an epic narrative summary.

### Why We’re Doing It:

This allows DMs to instantly share a polished, cinematic combat recap on Discord — creating viral word-of-mouth growth for the platform.

---

### Tier Distribution

**Free:**

* System logs only
  (Plain text like: “Goblin took 5 damage.”)

**Member:**

* One-click epic story generation at combat end via Gemini API

# 2. AI Content Generation (AI Generators)

This is where users will gladly pay $3 — and where your API costs are justified.

---

## Advanced Loot & Smart Treasure System

### What We Will Do:

We will enhance the existing loot generator by adding:

* Estimated Gold Value
* Required Crafting Materials
* Suggested CR (Challenge Rating) drop source

Additionally, after combat ends, the system will instantly generate thematic loot based on the defeated monster type.

### Why We’re Doing It:

DMs often find items but get stuck on:

* “How much would this sell for?”
* “What materials are required to craft this?”

These extra layers of detail make your app significantly more competitive.

---

### Tier Distribution

**Free:**

* Only 3 basic item generations per day
* No price estimate
* No material list
* No CR information

**Member:**

* Unlimited generation
* Price estimation
* Crafting materials list
* Monster-specific dynamic generation (Smart Treasure fully unlocked)

## Campaign & Party Cloud Synchronization

### What We Will Do:

We will store:

* Created parties
* Combat sessions
* Generated loot

inside a cloud database.

### Why We’re Doing It:

People don’t want their data to disappear when:

* They close the browser
* They switch devices
* They log in from their phone

For a $3 SaaS product, secure and persistent data is a fundamental promise.

---
